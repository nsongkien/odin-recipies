Cơm gà

Description

A dish containing boiled chicken with rice cooked with that boiled chicken stock.

Best use with cooked fish sauce - but can use soy sauce as an alternative.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (thighs or whole)
  • Rice
  • Curry powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Fish sauce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash the rice under cold water, let it sulk in water for 1 hour
  2. Wash your chickens, boil them from cold water for 15 minutes, add in a pinch of salt to the boiling water
  3. Rinse the water from your rice, mix in half a spoon of curry powder into it
  4. Sauté the garlic with hot oil
  5. Fry the rice until it turns transparent
  6. Add the rice into the rice cooker with the chicken stocks
  7. Serve with fish sauce or soy sauce plus chillies or hot sauces
